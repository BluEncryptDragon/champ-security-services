Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13/07/2026

Champ Security Services ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our security services.

This Privacy Policy has been prepared with consideration of the requirements of the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and the Information Privacy Principles (IPPs) that govern how organisations handle personal information.

1. About Champ Security Services

Champ Security Services provides professional security services including security guarding, mobile patrols, property security, and related security solutions.

Our website allows potential clients and members of the public to learn about our services and submit enquiries regarding security requirements.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it to us, including when you submit an enquiry through our website.

This information may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your company or organisation details
  • The details of your enquiry or security requirements
  • Any other information you choose to provide

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for the purpose of responding to enquiries, providing services, communicating with clients, and operating our business.

3. How We Use Your Information

Personal information collected by Champ Security Services may be used for purposes including:

  • Responding to enquiries and requests for information
  • Providing quotes, proposals, and security service information
  • Communicating with current or potential clients
  • Delivering and managing security services
  • Maintaining business records
  • Improving our website and services
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

4. Information Security

Champ Security Services takes reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

We use appropriate administrative and technical measures designed to protect information provided through our website and business systems.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted online.

5. Website Hosting and Third-Party Services

Our website may be hosted using third-party website hosting providers. These providers may process limited technical information required to deliver website services.

Where third-party services are used to support website functionality, forms, communications, analytics, or business operations, those providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

We take reasonable steps to ensure third-party providers used by Champ Security Services handle information appropriately.

6. Enquiry Forms

Information submitted through our website enquiry form is used only for the purpose of responding to your request and discussing potential security services.

You should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive personal information through website forms.

If you need to provide sensitive information, we recommend discussing the appropriate method of communication with us directly.

7. Disclosure of Information

Champ Security Services does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may disclose information where necessary:

  • To provide requested services
  • To trusted service providers assisting with business operations
  • Where required by law or legal obligations
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of individuals or organisations

8. Retention of Information

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including business, legal, accounting, and operational requirements.

When information is no longer required, reasonable steps will be taken to securely remove or destroy it.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you may have the right to request:

  • Access to personal information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Information about how your personal information is handled

To make a privacy request, please contact us using the details below.

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality, understand website usage, and enhance user experience.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
