What I bring to a site

Every environment is different. These are the disciplines that carry across all of them.

01

Static & Site Guarding

Long-form, single-point responsibility for a site — construction, commercial, and residential — where consistency and visible presence do the work of prevention.

02

Mobile & Night Patrol

Route-based patrol across large precincts and multi-site portfolios, including alarm arming, lock-up/unlock procedures, and perimeter checks after hours.

03

Crime Scene Guarding

Scene integrity and access control in direct support of New Zealand Police, maintaining chain of custody until scenes are formally released.

04

Critical Infrastructure

Fuel storage facilities, telecommunications infrastructure, marina and port assets — sites where a lapse has consequences well beyond the fence line.

05

Government & Public Sector

Front-of-house and internal security for public agency buildings, courts, and service centres, where the public and staff both need to feel safe.

06

Specialist & VIP Operations

Vetted, briefed operational work alongside Police and Secret Services | allied protective agencies for high-profile events and search operations, under strict confidentiality.

07

Healthcare & Hospital Security

Emergency Department and ward security, including patient restraint support, monitoring of high-risk patients, and de-escalation of aggressive or distressed individuals in a clinical setting.

08

Conflict De-escalation

Calm, controlled response to volatile situations — from aggressive patients to confrontations on patrol — prioritising safety for staff, patients, and the public over force.
